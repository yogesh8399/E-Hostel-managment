About NIT KURUKSHETRA

National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra is a public engineering institute located in Kurukshetra. In December 2008, it was accredited with the status of Institute of National Importance. It is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology established and administered by Government of India. National Institute of Technology (NIT) Kurukshetra, ranks at 43 among top engineering colleges in India by NIRF 2018. The institute offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA and MBA programs as well as a doctoral program in Humanities, Engineering, and Sciences. Since 2015, the placement statistics have shown consistent growth with overall percentage of the student getting placed shooting up to beyond 95%. Describing the placement statistics of 2019, 26.56% of students received more than 10 LPA packages, 61.25% of students received the placement package worth between 6-10 LPA, 43.17% received the package between 4-6 LPA and 3.87% of students received the package less than 4 LPA.t offers BTech, MTech, MBA, MCA, PhD and certificate courses in various specialisations. These courses are offered through 11 departments and four schools
